Regular Expression na namma vanga pora input ena format la iruaknum nu solurathu 

syntax : /^..$/

1. ^ - starting 
2. $ - Ending
3. [a-zA-Z0-9] - sequence of char and num 
4. [a-zA-Z0-9]+ [] - use + and give a space 
5. [a-zA-Z0-9]+@ - use + and add a @ character
6. {2,} - andha sequence la min number evlo irukanum , in here min 2 and ( , ) for above 2
